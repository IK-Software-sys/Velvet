The Rules of Velvet

Welcome to Velvet, a duel of wits and arrangement.

  • Objective: Be the first to arrange all cards in your hand into valid sets (melds).
  • Melds: A valid set is either 3 or 4 cards of the same rank (e.g., three 7s) OR a sequence of 3+ consecutive cards of the same suit (e.g., 4, 5, 6 of Hearts).
  • On Your Turn: You must draw one card from either the hidden deck or the discard pile.
  • Ending Your Turn: You must discard one card from your hand to the discard pile.
  • Victory: Once all cards in your hand form valid melds, click the "Declare Victory" button to claim your triumph.

Your hand will be automatically sorted to aid your dark quest.
