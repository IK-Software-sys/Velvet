Victorian Rummy

The Rules of Engagement

Objective: Form your cards into valid "Melds" (Sets or Runs) and empty your hand before your opponent.


Sets: 3 or 4 cards of the same rank, but different suits (e.g., 7♠, 7♥, 7♦).

Runs: 3 or more consecutive cards of the exact same suit (e.g., 4♣, 5♣, 6♣).


Jokers: Wildcards that can substitute for any card to complete a meld.


Turn Flow:
1. Draw a card from the Deck or the Discard Pile.
2. (Optional) Form melds.
3. Discard one card to end your turn.
