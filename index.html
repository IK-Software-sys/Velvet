The Rules of Rummy

  • Objective: Meld all 10 cards into valid Sets and Sequences.
  • Sequences (Runs): 3 or more consecutive cards of the same suit (e.g., 4♠, 5♠, 6♠).
  • Sets: 3 or 4 cards of the same rank but different suits (e.g., 7♥, 7♦, 7♣).
  • Jokers (j.jpg): Wildcards that can substitute for any missing card in a meld.
  • Turn Phase: First, Draw a card from the Deck or Discard pile. Then, Discard one card from your hand.
  • Winning: When all your cards form valid melds, click DECLARE.
