Victorian Rummy

The Path to Victory

  • The Objective: Meld all your cards into valid Sets (3-4 cards of the same rank) or Runs (3+ cards in consecutive order of the same suit).
  • The Turn: Draw one card from the deck or discard pile. Then, discard one card.
  • Jokers: The Jester acts as a wild card, filling in missing pieces of your melds.
  • Winning: When all cards in your hand are part of a meld and you discard your final deadwood, press Declare Win to conquer your opponent.
